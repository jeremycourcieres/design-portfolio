to the outside world.

SMA TO UFL
ANTENNA

During the field test an issue
occured where the user played around
with the antenna rod a spun it around.

This resulted in the cable severing internally.
This is how the challenge was tackled.

Parallel faces highlighted in red (88°). This allows maximum compression between the plate and the case.

The plate is restricted from all movement. This will stop the cable antenna from spinning itself and allow it to stay in place.

The SMA and the antenna plate are fused together using a bonding loctite retaining compound. To retain the IP the antenna plate has a O-ring seal.
